67. Does the Community monitor water quality for drinking, recreational, and ecological purposes and have a system to respond to any water quality issues in a timely manner?
TRUE
Yes, Manatee County, where Anna Maria resides, does monitor water quality for drinking, recreational, and ecological purposes and has a system to respond to any water quality issues in a timely manner.
Based on Manatee County‚Äôs 2020 Water Quality Report, Manatee County met all EPA and State drinking water health standards and additionally the Manatee County Water Purification Plant uses the multiple barrier approach to make sure that the water is safe. The approach used by the plant includes source protection, optimized particle removal at the purification plant and appropriate disinfection.
