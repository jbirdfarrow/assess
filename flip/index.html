Introduction

We cannot improve what we do know. Therefore,we begin by establishing a baseline for your community by completing a series of questions, based on themes in sustainability. The Blue Community Assessment app is a paperless process inside an easy to edit cloud-based virtual sustainability assessment ecosystem for destination communities. It allows the work of data collection, entry and update to be distributed across a team. All destinations are assigned a team color. When assessors log in, the system already knows that team they belong to and which criteria they are permitted to edit/update/access. Once all 133 questions have been given an initial answer, 100% of baseline will be complete. From that moment forward, everything is measured and tracked based on updates provided to the assessment over time, activating "Historical Data Modules" (more on this later).

We aim to continue building our ecosystem until the end of time. With this in mind, we can collaboratively support one another only when there remains 100% honesty in everything we report. All edits made to the destination data are time stamped and the change becomes attributed to the person making the change. This data is only viewable by the members belonging to the same team. This change becomes archived to our database and recorded as a new change within the blockchain. The blue community blockchain keeps a record of every change which happens to the data over time, beginning from the date baseline was established. We aim to challenge the negative impacts of tourism by providing a place where destination communities can collaboratively measure, report, track and analyze local sustainability efforts, as a team. Each team is assigned a color which represents the destination members of that team have permissions to edit, update or contribute to.
